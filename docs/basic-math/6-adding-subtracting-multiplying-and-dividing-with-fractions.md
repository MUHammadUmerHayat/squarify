## Question One

$
  \LARGE \frac{5}{7} + \frac{1}{7}
$

## Question One Step:

>

## Question Three:

$
  \LARGE \frac{7}{8} - \frac{1}{8}
$

## Question Three Steps:

> ### 1. Subtract the numerators and keep the denominator the same
>
> $
  \LARGE \frac{7}{8} - \frac{1}{8} = \frac{6}{8}
$

> ### 2. Reduce the fraction by dividing the numerator and denominator by 2
>
> $
  \LARGE \frac{6}{8} = \frac{3}{4}
$

## Question Three Answer:

$
  \LARGE \frac{3}{4}
$
